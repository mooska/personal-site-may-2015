A LITTLE ABOUT MYSELF

Past

I grew up in a southeastern farm town called Gallipolis. My interest for computers actually stemmed from Dungeons and Dragons. From D&D, I fell in love with Everquest. From EQ, I fell in love with computer performance and from that, I probably spent too much of my parents money.

Present

Nowadays, I find myself constantly looking at web design, keeping current with modern trends and, at the same time, appreciating the innovation of others. On top of that, I enjoy picking up concepts from others than implementing them in what I do from scratch. I find that is the best method for strengthening my skills and producing work I am truely proud of.

Future

My goal is to become one of the best front end developers, period. I don't plan on ever slowing down when it comes to learning new technologies, languages, and trends. I want to be a competent artist one day, capable of creating more than my current stick figures in paint. Most of all, I want to solve real problems and create solutions that have an effect on the web development ecosystem and people all over the world.
